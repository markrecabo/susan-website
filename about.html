About Susan Gleaton

Susan is a Registered Dietitian with certifications in Integrative and Functional Medical Nutrition Therapy who really understands the importance of digestive health and specializes in all types of digestive problems such as heartburn, gas, bloating, cyclic vomiting and chronic constipation or diarrhea, or perhaps alternating between the two.

Through her own struggles with lifelong constipation, gas, bloating, and a desire to help her clients, Susan discovered a method that has successfully helped many achieve digestive normalcy with her proven, evidence-based approach…even when they have had digestive problems for years.   Seeing her clients regain their health and experience normal digestion is truly a joy to Susan and keeps her propelled to tell more and more people about this under-utilized approach. Susan lives north of the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex on a thriving, busy homestead raising grass fed cows and pastured chickens.  She loves to prepare simple and healthy meals from scratch for her family, friends, and an ever-increasing tribe of jubilant grandchildren.
