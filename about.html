About Susan Gleaton

Hi! I’m Susan Gleaton, Registered Dietitian and Functional Medicine Nutrition Therapist. I look for the underlying causes of health problems so that they can be corrected.

I think digestive health is critical to our well-being and have devoted many hours figuring out how to help people with all types of digestive conditions such as heartburn/GERD, constipation, diarrhea, SIBO, IBS, Crohn’s and Colitis.

I’ve successfully helped many people achieve digestive normalcy with a proven, evidence-based approach…even when they have had digestive problems for years.

Seeing my clients experience normal digestion and regain their health is truly a joy to me, and I really put my all into helping every client that crosses my path.

My family and I live on a thriving, busy homestead north of Dallas where we raise grass fed cows and pastured chickens. We also have dogs, cats and guineas, each with their own role to play. I love preparing simple and healthy meals from scratch for my family, friends, and a growing tribe of jubilant grandchildren.
